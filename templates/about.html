TAMILNADU URBAN FINANCE AND INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION LIMITED(TUFIDCO)

The decadal growth rate of India's urban population demonstrates the fast-growing demand for various infrastructures, including basic amenities in the cities. The Government of Tamil Nadu has taken proactive measures in addressing this problem by establishing Tamil Nadu Urban Finance and Infrastructure Development Corporation (TUFIDCO) on 20.03.1990 under the Companies Act, 1956 with an aim of enhancing the quality of life for urban residents by providing basic infrastructure facilities in urban local bodies.

Vision and Mission

The vision of TUFIDCO is “to develop basic infrastructure in urban local bodies to the satisfactory level of inhabitants with sustainable facilities that attracts investments for the growth of the urban local bodies”.

Objectives of TUFIDCO

  1. To extend financial assistance for developmental schemes to urban local bodies / parastatal agencies.
  2. To undertake various infrastructure development schemes in collaboration with urban local bodies or with Government agencies.
  3. To finance various schemes such as basic services, asset creation and poverty alleviation for a better standard of living of all the people
