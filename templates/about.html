TAMILNADU URBAN FINANCE AND INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION LIMITED(TUFIDCO)

The decadal growth rate of India's urban populaticn demonstrates the fast-growing demand for various infrastructures, including basic amenities in the cities. The Government of Tamil Nadu has taken proactive measures in addressing this problem by establishing Tamil Nadu Urban Finance and Infrastructure Development Corporation (TUFIDCO) on 20.03.1990 under the Companies Act, 1956 with an aim of enhancing the quality of life for urban residents by providing basic infrastructure facilities in urban local bodies.
